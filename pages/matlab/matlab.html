CRT Filter

During my studies of signals and systems, I learned that you can replicate CRT television like effects using simple filter designs.

On the left is the original image of niko, the middle image is that same image passed through a high pass filter, giving it a grainy and distorted feeling and the right image is the original image passed through a low pass filter. Giving it a blurry feeling.

DFT and Music

When studying how efficient fourier transform algorithms are, its important that you understand the context in which theyre used.

I decided to study how efficient the FFT (Fast Fourier Transform) was over the DFT (Discrete Fourier Transform) by implementing both in MATLAB and applying them to a song to see how long they would take.

And what better song than feint operation from virtua cop 3? a classic!

DTFT of Filters

When designing filters, its often best you perform a fourier transform of the filter itself in order to make sure its actually targeting the frequencies you want to remove or amplify.

To properly analyze a filter, I used a very noisy audio file, and designed a series of band stop filters in order to attempt to clean it. However, this required I perform a DTFT on the filters themselves to make sure they were actually working.

The result was that the recovered audio was perfectly serviceable! You could say that were the right men in the right place ;-)
